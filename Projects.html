Some of my favorite projects! (Popcode snapshot links or Github Pages)

  • Playlist - A blank music playlist where you can add your own songs!
  • Tic Tac Toe - Virtual Tic Tac Toe so you don't have to waste paper or download an app!
  • Crack the Safe Game - A game where you have to crack a safe, then face a surprise!
  • Pittsburgh Steelers Fanpage - A fanpage for my favorite National Football League team, the Pittsburgh Steelers!
  • Make your own Playlist - A blank playlist where you can add your favorite songs!
  • Robert Robot - This is a virtual adventure where you have to shut down the robot before he encounters an error!
  • RockPaperScissors - This is a classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, but with a twist! This game is against the computer, with no way to tell what it will pick!
  • Fortune Teller - Get your (not very accurate) fortune told by the Fortune Teller! She uses a MadLib type function and a random number generator to tell your fortune!
